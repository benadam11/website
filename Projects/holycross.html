Holy Cross Tucson

Agency: Freelance
Roles: UI / UX / Art Direction

←Back to projects

Holy Cross Tucson needed a logo and print materials for a finance campaign, as well as branding, print materials, and a wall graphic for their childrens ministry. I developed a logo and color pallete for their capital campaign and used those assets in the branding of the childrens ministry.

Pete, the lead pastor at Holy Cross, communicated that he wanted to represent their ministry in a fresh new way, with clean design. The logo for the campaign is a litteral translation of their tagline "A church to call home" with an illustation of the building and the shadow of a home.
